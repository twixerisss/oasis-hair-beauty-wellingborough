The Salon · Floor Wellingborough
“We take our time. Every cut, colour and treatment is shaped around the client in front of us — never rushed, always considered, finished with care.

Oasis Hair & Beauty is a full-service salon on Doddington Road in the heart of Wellingborough — a calm, welcoming space where every appointment feels like the only one of the day. Our experienced team works across cutting, colouring, highlights, hair removal and beauty treatments, for men and women alike.

Whether it's a polished blow-dry for the working week, a full colour change, or a complete bridal package for the day itself, every treatment is delivered in a relaxed, refined environment — never overbooked, never rushed.
